The story behind VSP

Three decades of trust, precision, and promoter-first execution.

VSP Capital Advisors began in 1990, when CA Vishnu Gupta started building the banking relationships that still anchor our lender network today.

The firm is now led by CA Prakhar Gupta (AIR 22): one team, one desk, one process, across 30+ cities. From a one-desk practice in Indore to a national debt syndication firm — the lender relationships came first; everything else was built on them.

What we do

One desk, two doors.

Most clients need one door — debt syndication and project finance, the core of what we do, built over three decades of bank and NBFC relationships. A smaller number need the second door: private equity and IPO advisory.

Debt Syndication

Structuring and placing debt with the right lender, at the right price, for promoters across India — backed by 100+ bank and NBFC relationships.

Project Finance

End-to-end financial structuring for real estate, hospitality, industrial, and infrastructure projects, from due diligence to disbursal.

Private Equity & IPO Advisory

A growing second door for founders ready to raise equity capital, not just debt — the same desk designs the structure before we approach anyone.
