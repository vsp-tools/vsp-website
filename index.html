The story behind VSP

VSP Capital Advisors began in 1990, when CA Vishnu Gupta started building the banking relationships that still anchor our lender network today. The firm is now led by CA Prakhar Gupta (AIR 22): one team, one desk, one process, across 30+ cities.

What we do

One desk, two doors. Most clients need one door — debt syndication and project finance, the core of what we do, built over three decades of bank and NBFC relationships. A smaller number need the second door — private equity and IPO advisory, a capability we're building for founders ready to raise equity, not just debt.
